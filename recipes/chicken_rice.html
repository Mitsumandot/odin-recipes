Chicken and Rice

Description

This recipe is a very simple yet delicious one ! it doesnt require a lot of ingredients, but the result is gorgeously delicious and you wont regret going those steps !

Required ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Basmati rice
  • soy sauce
  • natural unsweet yogurt
  • salt, pepper and your usual spices

Steps

  1. First, use the yogurt, spices, and soy sauce to marinate the chicken breast (thinly sliced)
  2. wash the rice multiple time
  3. use one cup of rice, and two cups of water to cook it (if more rice, adjust considering it)
  4. Cook the chicken using olive oil for enhanced flavour
  5. at the end, you can serve the chicken and rice mixed, or, as i prefer, separeted.
